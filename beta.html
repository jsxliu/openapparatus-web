OpenApparatus Classroom Beta-Testing Program

We believe that high-fidelity physics equipment should not be locked behind a $300 commercial price tag. To support STEM educators, OpenApparatus is currently manufacturing and donating limited quantities of our state-of-the-art Dual-Tube Lenz's Law Demonstrators directly to public school classrooms.

How it works:

  1. Apply below to request a class set (subject to manufacturing availability).
  2. We manufacture and ship the fully assembled hardware to your school at zero cost.
  3. Your students use the tactile models to master electromagnetic induction.
  4. You provide us with 5 minutes of feedback to help us engineer the next iteration.

Note: We currently prioritize fulfillment for Title I schools and underfunded public districts.
